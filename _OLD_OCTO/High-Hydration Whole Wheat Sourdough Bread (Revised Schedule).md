### **Day 2: Dough Mixing, Folds, and Cold Proof**

**(Active Work: ~75 minutes total. All work finishes by 1:05 PM.)**

|**Time (EST)**|**Step**|**Action**|
|---|---|---|
|**05:50 AM**|**3. Autolyse**|In a large bowl, mix the autolyse ingredients until thoroughly combined. Cover.|
|06:00 AM|**Rest**|Cover and leave the dough untouched for 30 minutes.|
|06:30 AM|**Autolyse Fold**|Perform a bowl fold: Grab a section with a wet hand, lift, and press into the middle. Repeat until the dough tightens. Cover.|
|06:40 AM|**Rest**|Cover and leave the dough untouched for 90 minutes.|
|**08:10 AM**|**4. Final Dough Mix**|With a wet hand, spread the preferment onto the dough. Add the salt ($17 \text{g}$) and water ($45 \text{g}$), and mix until fully incorporated. Transfer to a 6-quart container and mark the height. Cover.|
|08:20 AM|**Rest**|Leave dough untouched for 30 minutes.|
|**08:50 AM**|**5. Bowl Fold (1st)**|Fold the dough by grabbing a section from one side, lifting it, and pressing it down. Repeat 8-12 times.|
|08:55 AM|**Rest**|Allow the dough to rest for 30 minutes.|
|**09:25 AM**|**6. Coil Fold (2nd)**|With wet hands, reach under the dough, stretch the middle upward until it releases, and allow it to coil on itself. Rotate $90^{\circ}$ and repeat.|
|09:30 AM|**Rest**|Allow the dough to rest for 30 minutes.|
|**10:00 AM**|**7. Lamination Fold (3rd)**|Dump the dough onto a lightly moistened surface, gently stretch into a large rectangle. Fold it like a letter, then roll it up tightly. Return to the container.|
|10:10 AM|**8. Bulk Fermentation**|Cover the dough and let it ferment until it has risen approximately $\mathbf{1.5}$ times its starting height ($\sim2$ hours).|
|**12:10 PM**|**9. Divide & Pre-Shape**|Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and divide into two equal pieces. Gently form rounds, cover, and rest seam-side down for 10 minutes.|
|**12:25 PM**|**10. Final Shape & Cold Proof**|Gently shape the dough into _b√¢tards_ or _boules_ and place in well-floured bannetons. Place in the refrigerator, loosely covered, to chill.|

### **Day 3: Baking**

**(The loaves will have cold-proofed for $\sim18$ hours.)**

|**Time (EST)**|**Step**|**Action**|
|---|---|---|
|**06:00 AM**|**11. Preheat Oven**|Preheat your oven to $500^{\circ}\text{F}$ for 1 hour. Place Dutch ovens (or baking stone/steel and cast iron pan) inside to preheat.|
|**07:00 AM**|**12. Bake (Covered)**|Remove chilled loaves from the refrigerator and turn them out into the hot baking vessels. Quickly score and cover. Place back in oven. Bake for $\mathbf{20}$ minutes.|
|07:20 AM|**Bake (Uncovered)**|Reduce heat to $475^{\circ}\text{F}$, remove the lids, and bake for an additional $\mathbf{30}$ minutes.|
|07:50 AM|**13. Finish Baking**|Turn off the oven and leave the loaves inside for an additional $\mathbf{30}$ to $\mathbf{60}$ minutes to monitor for desired color/crust.|
|**08:20 AM**|**14. Cool**|Remove the bread from the oven. Cool completely on a rack before slicing.|
