### Wall sconces

To effectively illuminate a bathroom mirror with sconces, they should be mounted ==about 60-65 inches from the floor to the center of the sconce, which is roughly at eye level==. For a balanced look when using two sconces, they should be spaced 28-36 inches apart, or roughly at shoulder width
